Programmes Offered

B.A. English Language and Literature

A comprehensive undergraduate programme focusing on major literary works, critical theories, and the nuances of the English language.

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Eligibility: Pass in Plus Two or equivalent examination.
  • Core Areas: British Literature, American Literature, Postcolonial Studies, Linguistics, Literary Theory.

M.A. English Language and Literature

An advanced postgraduate programme designed for in-depth study and research in specialized areas of English studies.

  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Eligibility: B.A. in English or a related discipline with required marks.
  • Specializations: Cultural Studies, Eco-criticism, Modernist Literature, Research Methodology.

Department Activities

Literary Seminars

Hosting national and international seminars with eminent scholars to foster academic discourse.

Creative Writing Workshops

Conducting workshops to nurture the creative writing talents of our students in poetry, fiction, and drama.

Film & Literary Club

Screening classic and contemporary films, followed by critical discussions on their literary adaptations.
